# Dispatch as a Service PRD

## Purpose
Provide the configuration and communication interface between Auric Works, a profile, installed cartridges, and the institution's Terminal.

## Capabilities
- Build and update institutional and personal profiles
- Configure cartridge behavior
- Ingest private context
- Route tasks to models and experts
- Generate and deliver intelligence
- Coordinate work between Auric Works and the institution
- Record evidence, decisions, outcomes, and cost

## Boundary
Dispatch may be invisible as the internal engine or offered as a branded private brain/configuration service inside a Terminal.
