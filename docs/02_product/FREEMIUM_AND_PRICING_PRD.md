# Freemium and Pricing PRD

## Free forever
- Public profiles
- Bi-daily Brief
- Public market search and feed
- Basic peer and opportunity signals
- Profile claim/correction
- Basic startup profile and improvement signals
- Network observation

## Low-friction paid tier
- Private profile and memory
- Role and goal refinement
- Saved watchlists
- Limited uploads
- Ask-your-institution/startup interface
- More precise lenses and alerts
- Bounded monthly compute

## Usage and execution
- Premium model deliberation
- Document processing
- Board and investor materials
- Diligence
- Pilot administration
- Integration orchestration
- Expert review
- Participation and portfolio administration where legally structured

## Pricing rule
Do not charge merely for existence in the market. Charge for persistence, private context, work, and outcomes.
