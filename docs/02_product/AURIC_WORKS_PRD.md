# Auric Works PRD

## Purpose
Operate the market and provide the human, capital, partnership, integration, and management capacity that software alone cannot.

## Service surfaces
- Managed innovation function
- Startup operating support
- Research and editorial authority
- Integration orchestration
- Pilot administration
- Investment/participation support through compliant structures
- Expert network
- Cartridge development and acquisition

## Product boundary
Auric Works services are invoked by Dispatch workflows and Terminal users but remain distinguishable from automated platform outputs.
