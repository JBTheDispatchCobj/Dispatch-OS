# The Auric PRD

## Promise
A timeless, remarkably brief, personalized signal every two days plus a searchable market feed.

## Four formats
1. **The Brief** — three to five high-value items in under one minute.
2. **The Market** — searchable, ranked, sourced feed.
3. **The Network** — relevant peer activity, discussion, collaboration, and actions.
4. **The $2 Bill** — a small, immediately useful innovation or operating improvement.

## Content principle
Create one base Intelligence Object, then apply institution, role, person, cartridge, and channel lenses.

## Channels
- Email
- Terminal
- Manual/editorial issue
- Web feed
- 35-second video
- Audio
- Memo
- Board note

## Success metric
Actions and profile refinement created—not content volume.
