\# Purpose

Volume VI defines The Auric Engine.

The Auric is not a CMS.

It is not a newsletter.

It is not social media automation.

It is the intelligence publication engine for Dispatch.

Its purpose is to transform operational knowledge into useful intelligence for the correct audience at the correct time.

Content is not the product.

Knowledge is not the product.

Operational improvement is the product.

Publications simply transport operational intelligence.

Every publication should increase someone's ability to make a better decision.

\---

\# Core Principle

Dispatch creates knowledge.

The Auric distributes knowledge.

The Auric never creates truth.

It renders truth into useful intelligence.
