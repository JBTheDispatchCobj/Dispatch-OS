\---

\# Purpose

The Knowledge Graph is the permanent representation of operational reality.

It is not a visualization.

It is not Neo4j.

It is not a database technology.

It is an architectural model describing how Dispatch understands the world.

\---

\# Philosophy

Dispatch does not store records.

Dispatch understands systems.

A traditional application stores rows.

Dispatch stores operational reality.

Reality consists of

Objects

↓

Relationships

↓

Evidence

↓

Truth

↓

Memory

↓

Time

↓

Outcomes

\---

\# Core Principle

Nothing exists alone.

Everything meaningful exists because of its relationship to something else.

Organizations exist because they employ people.

Products exist because organizations build them.

Investments exist because organizations fund companies.

Meetings exist because people interact.

Knowledge is relational.

\---

\# Why Graph Instead of Documents

Documents describe reality.

Graphs model reality.

Documents become stale.

Relationships evolve continuously.

Dispatch stores the relationships.

Documents become evidence.

\# The Four Graphs

Dispatch consists of four logical graphs.

Knowledge Graph

The canonical network of Objects and Relationships.

Truth Graph

Verified Claims, Facts, Evidence, and Confidence.

Operational Graph

Workflows, Decisions, Activities, and Outcomes.

Memory Graph

Institutional learning accumulated through execution.

These appear as one graph to the platform.

\# Graph Integrity

Integrity requires

No duplicate identity.

No orphan relationships.

No untraceable facts.

No invisible provenance.

No hidden assumptions.

Integrity precedes intelligence.

\=

×

×

Time

×

\# Design Rules

Relationships outweigh documents.

Truth outweighs opinions.

Memory outweighs prompts.

Evidence outweighs summaries.

Execution outweighs content.

Learning outweighs repetition.

\# Success Metrics

The graph succeeds when

Every object can explain itself.

Every relationship can justify itself.

Every recommendation traces to facts.

Every publication traces to intelligence.

Every workflow traces to graph state.

Every correction improves future decisions.

\---

\# Acceptance Criteria

Implementation is complete when

\- The graph models reality rather than documents.  
\- Objects and relationships remain first-class.  
\- Truth remains explainable.  
\- Profiles are assembled dynamically.  
\- Time is preserved.  
\- Intelligence derives from knowledge.  
\- Execution feeds learning.  
\- Every subsystem consumes the same graph.
