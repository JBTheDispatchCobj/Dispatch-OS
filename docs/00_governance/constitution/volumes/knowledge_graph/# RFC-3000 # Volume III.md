\# Purpose

Volume III specifies the Knowledge Graph.

The Knowledge Graph is the permanent representation of operational reality inside Dispatch.

It transforms disconnected information into institutional intelligence.

The Kernel executes.

The Graph understands.

\---

\# Philosophy

Dispatch is not document-centric.

Dispatch is graph-centric.
