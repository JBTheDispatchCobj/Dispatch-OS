# Pilot Statement of Work Template

## Parties and Purpose
Identify parties, governing agreement, and bounded pilot objective.

## Deliverables
Enumerate environment, tenant, configured workflows, connectors, data loads, dashboards, training, support, and value report.

## Customer Responsibilities
Sponsor access, source data, approvals, SMEs, security/procurement responses, users, and timely decisions.

## Auric Responsibilities
Configuration, controlled operation, testing, support, audit evidence, and agreed documentation.

## Assumptions and Exclusions
No autonomous regulated decision, unapproved production write, external send, custom feature, or out-of-scope data.

## Schedule and Acceptance
Milestones, acceptance tests, remediation period, change control, and termination rights.

## Fees and Expenses
Implementation, platform, usage, pass-through, taxes, payment schedule, and approved expenses.

## Security, Data and Legal
Reference DPA, security exhibit, permitted models, retention, confidentiality, IP, and regulatory responsibility.

## Signatures
Authorized representatives only.
