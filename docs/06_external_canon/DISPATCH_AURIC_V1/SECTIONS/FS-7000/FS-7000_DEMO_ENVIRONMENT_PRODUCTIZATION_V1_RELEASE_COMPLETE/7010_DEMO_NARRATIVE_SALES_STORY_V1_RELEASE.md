# 7010 — Demo Narrative & Sales Story V1

## Purpose

Define a clear, repeatable story that communicates the product without overwhelming the audience with architecture.

## Core Narrative

“Dispatch turns fragmented institutional context into a governed operating system that can understand the institution, identify what matters, route the work, and prove the result.”

## Four Actions

1. Understand
2. Decide
3. Execute
4. Prove

## Recommended Demo Arc

### Act 1 — Understand

Open the Credit Union Terminal.

Show:

- institution profile
- executive graph
- vendor stack
- regulatory profile
- readiness
- risks

### Act 2 — Decide

Open opportunity queue.

Explain:

- why opportunities were detected
- value
- readiness
- evidence
- sponsor
- blockers

### Act 3 — Execute

Launch fintech pilot or participation workflow.

Show:

- routing
- agents
- evidence
- approvals
- tasks
- outputs

### Act 4 — Prove

Open evidence and audit trail.

Show:

- source documents
- approval history
- versioning
- board memo
- measurable outcome

## Audience Variants

### Credit Union Executive

Lead with:

- institutional visibility
- opportunity
- readiness
- board confidence
- operational leverage

### Regulator / Compliance

Lead with:

- evidence
- controls
- permissions
- audit trail
- human approval

### Fintech

Lead with:

- institutional readiness
- pilot path
- buyer mapping
- integration
- compliance

### Investor / Capital Partner

Lead with:

- market map
- institutional network
- opportunities
- participation
- deployment infrastructure

## Proof Points

Avoid unsupported market claims.

Use:

- fixture-based demonstrated outcomes
- workflow completeness
- evidence traceability
- reduced manual steps
- improved decision speed
- clearer institutional readiness

## Demo Close

End with:

- what was learned
- what action is ready
- what remains blocked
- who owns next step
- what evidence supports it

## Acceptance Tests

- Narrative fits within 20–30 minutes.
- Technical architecture appears only when relevant.
- Every claim is demonstrated.
- Audience-specific versions remain consistent.
- Close includes a concrete pilot next step.
