# RFC-11010 — Regulatory and Compliance Intelligence

Status: Draft 1.0

Owner: Auric Works

Authority: Dispatch Constitution and Volumes I–X

## Regulatory intelligence model

Authority → jurisdiction → regulated entity → rule/source → requirement → applicability → control → evidence → test → finding → remediation → approval → filing/exam.

## Coverage

NCUA, state credit-union regulators, CFPB, FinCEN, OFAC, Federal Reserve, FDIC, OCC, SEC, FINRA, FTC, state privacy and financial regulators, payment-network rules, and product-specific authorities.

## Output

Applicability maps, evidence checklists, control gaps, review packages, change alerts, and cited institutional guidance. Human compliance and legal review remain mandatory where required.
