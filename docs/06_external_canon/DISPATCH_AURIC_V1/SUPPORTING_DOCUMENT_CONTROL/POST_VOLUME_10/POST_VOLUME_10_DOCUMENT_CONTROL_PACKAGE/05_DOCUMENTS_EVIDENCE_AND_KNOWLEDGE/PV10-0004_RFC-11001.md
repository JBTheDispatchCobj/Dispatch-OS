# RFC-11001 — Institutional Intelligence Philosophy

Status: Draft 1.0

Owner: Auric Works

Authority: Dispatch Constitution and Volumes I–X

## Purpose

Institutional Intelligence turns fragmented public and private signals into explainable institutional understanding and executable next actions.

## Principles

- Institutions first; transactions are outcomes.
- Capabilities before vendors.
- Evidence before claims.
- Readiness before recommendations.
- Relationships before cold distribution.
- Private knowledge never leaks.
- Public knowledge compounds.
- Human authority remains explicit.
- Innovation is an operating lifecycle, not a directory.

## Mission alignment

Make institutions innovative and innovation institutional-grade.
