# RFC-11011 — Institutional Decision and Evidence Model

Status: Draft 1.0

Owner: Auric Works

Authority: Dispatch Constitution and Volumes I–X

## Decision object

Question, decision owner, authority, context, alternatives, evidence, assumptions, conflicts, risks, financial impact, regulatory impact, recommendation, approvals, decision, conditions, outcome, and retrospective.

## Evidence hierarchy

Primary authority and institution records → verified filings and official data → licensed data → credible publications → participant claims → model inference.

## Rule

Derived intelligence inherits the strictest visibility and lowest reliable confidence of its material dependencies.
