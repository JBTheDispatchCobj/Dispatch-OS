# Evidence, Confidence, and Freshness Rules

## Evidence tiers

Tier 1 official authority or institution system; Tier 2 verified filing or audited report; Tier 3 licensed/credible structured data; Tier 4 credible publication; Tier 5 participant claim; Tier 6 model inference.

## Required controls

Every fact stores source, retrieval time, effective date, evidence tier, confidence, expiration/review date, visibility, and transformation lineage. Conflicting facts remain visible until resolved. Time-sensitive facts decay according to domain policy.
