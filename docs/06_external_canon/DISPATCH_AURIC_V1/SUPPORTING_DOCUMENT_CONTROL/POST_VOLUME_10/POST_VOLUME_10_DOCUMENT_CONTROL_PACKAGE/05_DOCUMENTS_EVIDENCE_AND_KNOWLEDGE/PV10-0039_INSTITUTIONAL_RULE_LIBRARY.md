# Institutional Rule Library

## Rule families

1. Identity and deduplication.
2. Source authority, freshness, and confidence.
3. Public/private visibility and ownership.
4. Institution peer grouping and financial normalization.
5. Capability-to-product and vendor mapping.
6. Readiness scoring and gating.
7. Fit and opportunity scoring.
8. Regulatory applicability and evidence.
9. Procurement, pilot, and implementation gates.
10. Partnership and investment human-approval gates.
11. Relationship-strength and warm-path calculation.
12. Publication eligibility and private-data exclusion.
13. Outcome learning and score recalibration.
14. Cost, risk, and model-routing limits.

## Minimum rule contract

Rule ID, title, domain, purpose, trigger, inputs, conditions, calculation, outputs, confidence, authority, evidence, exceptions, escalation, owner, version, effective date, tests, and events.
