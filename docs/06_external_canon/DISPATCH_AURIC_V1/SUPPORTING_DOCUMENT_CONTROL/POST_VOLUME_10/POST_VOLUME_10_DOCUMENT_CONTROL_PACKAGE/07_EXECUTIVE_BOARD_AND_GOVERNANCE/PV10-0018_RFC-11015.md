# RFC-11015 — Acceptance, Evaluation, and Operational Readiness

Status: Draft 1.0

Owner: Auric Works

Authority: Dispatch Constitution and Volumes I–X

## V1 acceptance

- Canonical identities resolve across public sources.
- Institution, executive, fintech, product, and regulator profiles assemble dynamically.
- Capability taxonomy supports vendor-independent matching.
- Innovation lifecycle executes from problem through outcome.
- Readiness and fit scores are evidence-backed and explainable.
- Regulatory intelligence links authority to requirement, control, and evidence.
- Public/private projections remain isolated.
- Opportunities become workspaces and execution plans.
- Outcomes update profiles, relationships, scores, and publications.
- APIs, events, audit, tests, and runbooks exist.

## Operational KPI set

Profile completeness, identity-resolution accuracy, source freshness, evidence coverage, match precision, recommendation acceptance, time to qualified introduction, time to pilot, pilot success, deployment success, knowledge reuse, and private-data leakage incidents (target: zero).
