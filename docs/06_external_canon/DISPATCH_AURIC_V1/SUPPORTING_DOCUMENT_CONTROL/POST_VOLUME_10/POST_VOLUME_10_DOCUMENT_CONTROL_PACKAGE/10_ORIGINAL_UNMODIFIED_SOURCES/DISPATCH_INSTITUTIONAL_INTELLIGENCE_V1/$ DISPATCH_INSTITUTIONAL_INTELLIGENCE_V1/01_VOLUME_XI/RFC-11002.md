# RFC-11002 — Institution and Market Domain Model

Status: Draft 1.0

Owner: Auric Works

Authority: Dispatch Constitution and Volumes I–X

## Canonical domains

Institution, legal entity, charter, regulator, organization unit, branch, committee, executive, board member, member/customer segment, market, peer group, geography, financial period, operating metric, strategy, initiative, risk, control, technology, vendor, contract, product, and relationship.

Observed → Verified → Profiled → Participating → Collaborating → Piloting → Deploying → Investing → Leading → Historical.

## Required projections

Public profile, internal operating profile, executive view, board view, innovation view, vendor view, investor view, regulatory view, and market view.
