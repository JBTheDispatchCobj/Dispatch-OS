# Dispatch Institutional Intelligence V1

## Purpose

This package is the missing industry operating layer above Dispatch OS Volumes I–X. The first ten volumes define how Dispatch governs, remembers, reasons, executes, connects, and presents. Institutional Intelligence defines what the system must know about credit unions, fintech, financial infrastructure, lending, capital markets, regulation, compliance, innovation, and institutional operations.

## Product role

- **Auric Works** operates the company, services, partnerships, and capital activity.
- **The Auric** turns the institutional graph into publications and market intelligence.
- **[Institution] Terminal** presents each institution's profile, feed, products, network, readiness, workspaces, and private operating memory.
- **Dispatch** supplies orchestration, truth, objects, agents, rules, connectors, execution, and evidence.
- **Institutional Intelligence** supplies the domain ontology, market graph, rules, workflows, profiles, and decision systems that make the bridge between innovation and institutions operational.

## Definition of V1 complete

V1 is complete when the application can ingest a public institution, fintech, product, regulatory source, or relationship event; resolve it to canonical objects; update the graph; calculate readiness and fit; produce an evidence-backed Intelligence Object; and surface a recommended next action in the Terminal.
