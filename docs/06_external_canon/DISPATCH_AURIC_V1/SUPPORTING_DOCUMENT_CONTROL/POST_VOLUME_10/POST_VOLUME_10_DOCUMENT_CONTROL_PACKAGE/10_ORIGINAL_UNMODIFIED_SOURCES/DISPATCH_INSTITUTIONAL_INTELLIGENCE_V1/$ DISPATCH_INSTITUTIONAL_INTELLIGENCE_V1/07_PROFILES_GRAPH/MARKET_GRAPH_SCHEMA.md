# Institutional Market Graph Schema

## Node families

Institutions, people, committees, CUSOs, fintechs, vendors, products, capabilities, systems, regulators, associations, investors, funds, pilots, partnerships, investments, contracts, regulations, filings, publications, and events.

## High-value edges

owns, member_of, regulates, examines, employs, serves_on_board, advises, uses, integrates_with, provides, customer_of, partnered_with, piloted, implemented, invested_in, financed, participated_in, referred, introduced, sponsors, processes_for, settles_through, depends_on, competes_with, acquired, exited, approved, rejected, and influenced.

## Edge requirements

Source and target IDs, type, role, direction, start/end, evidence, confidence, visibility, owner, freshness, relationship strength, interaction history, outcomes, and introduction path.

## V1 graph queries

Who uses this provider? Who integrates with this core? Which institutions share this problem? Who can introduce these participants? Which pilots produced measurable outcomes? Which investors have operating relationships? Which regulations apply to this product and institution?
