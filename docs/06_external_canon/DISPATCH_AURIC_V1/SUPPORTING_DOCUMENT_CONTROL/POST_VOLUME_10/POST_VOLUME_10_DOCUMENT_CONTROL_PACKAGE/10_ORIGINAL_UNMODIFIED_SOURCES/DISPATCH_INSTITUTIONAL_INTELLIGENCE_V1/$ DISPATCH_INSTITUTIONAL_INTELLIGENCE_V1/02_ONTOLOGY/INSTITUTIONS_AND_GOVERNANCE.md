# Institutions and Governance Ontology

## Categories and inputs

- **Institution identity:** legal name, common name, charter, charter number, tax ID, regulator IDs, RSSD/NCUA identifiers, legal form, ownership, status, headquarters, geography.
- **Cooperative structure:** membership, field of membership, sponsor groups, voting, member eligibility, capital shares, patronage, service organization participation.
- **Governance:** board, committees, charters, authority, delegated limits, minutes, resolutions, conflicts, terms, elections, succession.
- **Organization:** executives, departments, business units, branches, service centers, reporting lines, responsibilities, headcount, budgets.
- **Strategy:** mission, strategic plan, priorities, initiatives, scorecards, risk appetite, investment appetite, innovation mandate.
- **Financial condition:** assets, loans, shares/deposits, capital/net worth, earnings, liquidity, delinquency, charge-offs, concentrations, peer group, trends.
- **Operations:** products, channels, systems, vendors, workflows, service levels, incidents, projects, exams, audits.
- **Relationships:** regulator, corporate CU, CUSOs, leagues, vendors, fintechs, investors, partners, peers, communities.
