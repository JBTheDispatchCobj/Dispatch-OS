# Institutional Intelligence Acceptance Tests

1. Resolve one institution from NCUA ID, legal name, website, and alias to one canonical Object.
2. Assemble a public institution profile with cited facts, confidence, and freshness.
3. Add private strategic priorities without changing public projection.
4. Map three fintech products to canonical capabilities.
5. Calculate institution readiness, fintech readiness, and mutual fit with explanations.
6. Identify a warm introduction path from the relationship graph.
7. Generate an Opportunity with evidence, risks, dependencies, and next action.
8. Convert the Opportunity into a pilot Workspace and Execution Plan.
9. Map at least one official regulatory source to an applicability candidate, control, and evidence checklist.
10. Generate one Auric publication and one Terminal briefing from the same Intelligence Object.
11. Record pilot outcome and verify profiles, relationship strength, readiness, and future recommendations update.
12. Prove private institution data never appears in public search, profile, API, or publication.
