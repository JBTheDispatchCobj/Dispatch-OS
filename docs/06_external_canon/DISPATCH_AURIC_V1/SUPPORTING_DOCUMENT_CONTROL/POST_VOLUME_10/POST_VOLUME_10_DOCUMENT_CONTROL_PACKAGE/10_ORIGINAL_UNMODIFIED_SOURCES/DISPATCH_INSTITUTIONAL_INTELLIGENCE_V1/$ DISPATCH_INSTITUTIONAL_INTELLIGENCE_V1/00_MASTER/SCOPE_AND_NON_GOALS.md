# Scope and Non-Goals

## In scope

Institutional identity, public profiles, products, capabilities, systems, people, relationships, financial condition, regulatory posture, innovation readiness, vendor and fintech readiness, opportunity detection, pilot and partnership execution, investment support, publications, and evidence-backed recommendations.

## Not V1

- Autonomous fiduciary, legal, regulatory, lending, or investment decisions.
- Replacement of institution systems of record.
- Unlicensed broker-dealer, investment-adviser, placement-agent, or legal activity.
- Full coverage of every jurisdiction and product variation.
- Perfect real-time integrations with all providers.
- Consumer-facing retail banking.

## V1 boundary

The product assembles intelligence, identifies gaps and fit, prepares work, routes approvals, records evidence, and supports human execution. Regulated decisions remain with authorized humans and institutions.
