# Institutional Profile Schemas

## Institution profile

Identity, charter/regulator, financial condition, peer group, leadership, governance, products, channels, customers/members, technology, vendors, CUSOs, initiatives, strategy signals, risk/compliance, relationships, innovation history, readiness, opportunities, evidence, freshness, and next actions.

## Fintech/vendor profile

Identity, ownership/funding, founders/leaders, products, capabilities, infrastructure roles, customers, integrations, regulated dependencies, security/compliance, commercial model, implementation, support, references, readiness, institution fit, relationships, risks, evidence, and next actions.

## Executive profile

Identity, current role, career graph, boards/committees, expertise, publications, initiatives, relationships, influence, interests, decisions, outcomes, trust, visibility, and opportunities.

## Product/capability profile

Definition, provider, target customer, problem, eligibility, economics, workflow, systems, data, risk, regulation, controls, integrations, evidence, adoption, performance, and alternatives.

## Regulator/authority profile

Jurisdiction, entities, products and activities supervised, sources, filings, exams, guidance, enforcement, contacts, relationships, changes, and applicability.
