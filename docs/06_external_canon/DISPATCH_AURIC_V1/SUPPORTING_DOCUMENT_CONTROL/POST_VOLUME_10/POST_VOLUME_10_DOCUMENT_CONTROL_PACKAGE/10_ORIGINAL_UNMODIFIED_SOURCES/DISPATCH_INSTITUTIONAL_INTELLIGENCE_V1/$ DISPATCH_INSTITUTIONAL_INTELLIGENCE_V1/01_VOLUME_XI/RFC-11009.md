# RFC-11009 — Readiness, Fit, and Opportunity Intelligence

Status: Draft 1.0

Owner: Auric Works

Authority: Dispatch Constitution and Volumes I–X

## Scores

Institution innovation readiness, operational capacity, technology readiness, data readiness, procurement readiness, compliance readiness, integration readiness, fintech institutional readiness, product fit, relationship fit, pilot readiness, investment readiness, and partnership readiness.

## Opportunity contract

Every opportunity includes participants, problem, capability, evidence, strategic alignment, readiness, expected impact, cost, risk, relationship path, dependencies, next action, owner, and confidence.

## Guardrail

A high-fit opportunity is not recommended for execution until minimum readiness thresholds are satisfied.
