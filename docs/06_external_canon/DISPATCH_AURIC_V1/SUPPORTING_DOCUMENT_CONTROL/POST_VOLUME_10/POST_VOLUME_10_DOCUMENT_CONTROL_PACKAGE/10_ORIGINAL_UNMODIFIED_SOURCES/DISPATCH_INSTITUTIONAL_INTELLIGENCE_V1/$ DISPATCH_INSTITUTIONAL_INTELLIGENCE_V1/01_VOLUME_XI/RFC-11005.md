# RFC-11005 — Cooperative Finance Model

Status: Draft 1.0

Owner: Auric Works

Authority: Dispatch Constitution and Volumes I–X

## Cooperative-finance objects

Credit union, corporate credit union, CUSO, league, association, shared service, shared branching, participation network, liquidity provider, cooperative investment vehicle, sponsor institution, member institution, and consortium.

## Cooperative mechanics

Ownership, membership, patronage, shared economics, governance rights, capital contribution, voting, service eligibility, participation, liquidity, network benefit, and member value.

## Objective

Model how cooperative institutions jointly source, evaluate, deploy, finance, and benefit from innovation.
