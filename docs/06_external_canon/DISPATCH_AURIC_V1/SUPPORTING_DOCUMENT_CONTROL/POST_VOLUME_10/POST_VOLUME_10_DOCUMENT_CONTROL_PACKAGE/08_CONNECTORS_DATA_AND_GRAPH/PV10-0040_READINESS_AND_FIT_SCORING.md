# Readiness and Fit Scoring Specification

## Institution readiness dimensions

Strategy, executive sponsorship, budget, governance, procurement, data, technology, integration, security, compliance, operations, change management, measurement, and relationship access.

## Fintech readiness dimensions

Product maturity, customer evidence, implementation capacity, security, compliance, financial stability, support, integrations, documentation, governance, references, scalability, and concentration risk.

## Fit dimensions

Problem/capability alignment, product eligibility, technology compatibility, regulatory compatibility, institution segment, relationship proximity, economics, timing, geography, implementation burden, risk, and expected outcome.

## Rules

Scores must expose evidence, missing inputs, confidence, formula version, and gating failures. Composite scores never hide component failures. Critical compliance or security failures may override high commercial fit.
