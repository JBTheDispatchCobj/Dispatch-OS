# External Source Catalog

This catalog lists target source families. Each connector requires a separate source contract before production use.

## Credit-union regulatory and financial
- NCUA quarterly Call Report and Profile data
- NCUA active institution/credit-union lists
- NCUA regulations, legal opinions, letters, guidance, enforcement, and board materials
- Federal Credit Union Act and amendments
- FFIEC data and guidance relevant to financial institutions and third parties
- State credit-union statutes, administrative codes, regulator guidance, enforcement, and institution lists
- NASCUS resources where licensed or publicly available
- Corporate credit-union disclosures and public reports

## Securities and private markets
- SEC EDGAR company filings
- Form D and amended Form D filings
- Investment Adviser Public Disclosure / Form ADV
- State securities filings where accessible
- Public fund and portfolio disclosures
- Company funding announcements

## Institution and executive
- Official credit-union websites
- Annual reports and financial statements
- Press releases
- Executive and board pages
- Job postings
- Conference speakers and public committee memberships
- Public corporate/CUSO registrations

## Startup, vendor, and product
- Official websites and product documentation
- Security, compliance, trust, and status pages
- Public API and integration documentation
- Public GitHub repositories
- Patent and trademark databases
- Funding announcements and SEC filings
- Customer announcements and case studies
- App stores and product-review sources, subject to terms

## News and publication
- Primary-source feeds and press releases
- Licensed or permitted RSS feeds
- Market publications and trade press
- Public web reporting for attributed summaries, comparisons, and transformative analysis

## Network-generated
- Profile claims and corrections
- Interest, save, comment, vote, referral, discussion, and collaboration events
- Pilot, integration, partnership, investment, and outcome data
- Expert annotations

## Connector types
- Direct API
- Scheduled bulk download
- RSS/Atom
- Webhook
- Browser/developer-tool assisted capture
- Manual upload
- Email forwarding
- Structured extraction from approved public pages
- Partner-provided feed
