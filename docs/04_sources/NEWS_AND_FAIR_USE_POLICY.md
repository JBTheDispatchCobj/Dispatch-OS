# News Ingestion and Publication Policy

Dispatch may aggregate facts and market reporting, but The Auric should not republish full copyrighted articles.

Default behavior:
- Preserve source attribution and canonical link.
- Extract entities, events, facts, and claims.
- Produce concise summaries and transformative institution/role-specific analysis.
- Avoid substituting for the original article.
- Use minimal quotation.
- Respect robots, licenses, API terms, paywalls, and takedown processes.
- Prefer primary sources for durable facts.

Legal review is required before broad commercial crawling or reuse of a publication's corpus.
